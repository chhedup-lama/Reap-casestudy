Identity & Access Management

[ Role-based access: Analyst, Senior Reviewer, Compliance Lead, Admin, Read-only. Least privilege principle. MFA enforced. ]

Secrets Management

[ No secrets in code. All credentials via secrets manager (AWS Secrets Manager / HashiCorp Vault). Rotation policy. ]
