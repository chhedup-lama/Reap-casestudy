Block 1 — Executive Framing

Success Criteria & KPIs

What success looks like across speed, quality, compliance, and platform reuse — and how it's measured.

Objective
Design an AI-assisted KYB Review Platform that reduces review time, improves decision quality, and ensures compliance — while building reusable infrastructure for future workflows.

Goals

01 — Business
Business Goals
  • Reduce KYB turnaround time
  • Improve onboarding conversion and time-to-activation
  • Lower cost per case via automation
  • Scalable, consistent cross-border onboarding
02 — Reviewer & Ops
User Goals
  • Eliminate manual document reading and reconciliation
  • Unified case view: data, evidence, and history
  • Faster decisions with clear AI-grounded reasoning
  • Fewer follow-up loops, better-quality asks
03 — Customer
Customer Goals
  • Fewer redundant or unclear document requests
  • Transparency into requirements and decisions
  • Minimal onboarding friction and delays
04 — Compliance & Control
Compliance Goals
  • Consistent, policy-aligned, and auditable decisions
  • Early detection of prohibited/restricted use cases
  • Complete evidence traceability and decision rationale
  • Reduce false negatives while managing false positives
05 — Platform
Platform Goals
  • Reusable document intelligence, retrieval, and reasoning
  • Configurable workflows across KYB/KYC/AML
  • API-first, multi-tenant, scalable architecture
  • Feedback loops for continuous model improvement
06 — AI / Copilot
AI Goals
  • Augment human reviewers — not replace them
  • Grounded, explainable recommendations with citations
  • Abstain or escalate in low-confidence scenarios
  • Consistent judgment across similar cases

Non-Goals & Design Principles

Explicit Non-Goals
  • ✗Fully autonomous decision-making — humans decide
  • ✗Black-box, non-explainable AI outputs
  • ✗Solving upstream sales or lead qualification systems
  • ✗Eliminating all false positives — not a realistic target
Design Principles
  • ✓Human-in-the-loop for all final decisions
  • ✓Evidence-backed, explainable outputs by default
  • ✓Safe fallbacks to manual review
  • ✓Policy-driven reasoning as the source of truth

Measurement Approach

Four complementary measurement methods that validate AI performance at each stage of deployment.

Method 01
Offline Evals
Golden datasets — extraction accuracy, retrieval quality, decision consistency.
Method 02
Scenario Testing
Edge-case coverage — low-confidence, conflicting evidence, abstention behavior.
Method 03
A/B Testing
Assisted vs. unassisted — review time, override rate, decision quality.
Method 04
Audit Sampling
Reviewer feedback loops feeding corrections back into model evaluation.
