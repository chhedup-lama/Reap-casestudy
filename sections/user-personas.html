Block 2 — Users & Workflow

User Personas & Stakeholders

Four personas interact with the KYB review system — two doing the work, two depending on its outcomes.

Primary Users  ·  Designed for
Primary · High Volume
KYB Analyst
Core operational user. Reviews merchant applications, validates documents, cross-checks entity data, flags risk signals, and prepares approval or escalation recommendations.
Day in the Life
  • Pull cases from queue
  • Review & validate submitted docs
  • Cross-check entity & ownership data
  • Identify gaps, flag risk signals
  • Draft follow-up requests
  • Escalate edge cases
Tools Today
CRM / Onboarding Case Mgmt Doc Viewer Policy PDFs External Checks Email Spreadsheets
Key Frustrations
  • Constant tab-switching across tools
  • Manual data extraction from docs
  • Slow policy & SOP lookup
  • Repetitive follow-up drafting
  • Rework from incomplete submissions
  • Low confidence on borderline cases
Needs from Product
  • Single workspace — all context in one place
  • Pre-extracted data with source citations
  • Gaps & inconsistencies surfaced automatically
  • Policy-grounded AI (not generic summaries)
  • Fast contextual follow-up drafts
  • Clear confidence scores + abstention
Good day looks like High case throughput, low rework, no avoidable escalations — with every decision traceable and audit-defensible.
Primary · Control Point
Compliance Lead / Senior Reviewer
Escalation handler and governance owner. Interprets policy in edge cases, oversees analyst decision quality, and is accountable for audit readiness across the entire review process.
Day in the Life
  • Review escalated & high-risk cases
  • Validate analyst recommendations
  • Interpret policy for edge cases
  • Monitor operational quality
  • Respond to audit & control queries
  • Refine SOPs, address bottlenecks
Tools Today
Case Mgmt Policy Repos Audit Logs Risk Systems Queue Dashboards Messaging
Key Frustrations
  • Escalations arriving without context
  • Inconsistent analyst documentation
  • Time reconstructing decision rationale
  • Black-box AI in regulated workflows
  • Incomplete evidence chains at audit
  • Recurring errors hard to diagnose
Needs from Product
  • Synthesized escalation view with full case context
  • Evidence → policy traceability on every decision
  • Override & rationale capture built in
  • Confidence scores & risk-based routing
  • Audit-ready logs: who, what, when, why
  • Quality & consistency oversight dashboards
Good day looks like Fewer preventable escalations, stronger control visibility — every decision explainable, every policy application consistent.
Secondary Users  ·  Outcome-Dependent
Risk Operations
Pipeline health & queue management
Monitors queue states, aging cases, escalation volumes, and reviewer throughput. Does not perform case reviews — accountable for operational flow and SLA adherence.
Key needs
  • Queue visibility by stage, priority, and age
  • SLA breach prediction & bottleneck alerts
  • Throughput & capacity dashboards
  • Escalation & rework trend monitoring
Onboarding / Sales
Upstream funnel & growth-critical dependency
Not involved in review decisions — but directly affected by KYB turnaround. Depends on predictable timelines, clear status visibility, and minimal applicant friction.
Key needs
  • Faster, more predictable time-to-onboard
  • Fewer document resubmission loops
  • Application status visibility
  • Reduced drop-off from onboarding friction
Design Implication
Design primarily for the KYB Analyst's point of work — single workspace, pre-surfaced evidence, policy-grounded AI. Ensure strong controls and traceability for the Compliance Lead, operational dashboards for Risk Operations, and faster downstream outcomes for Onboarding. The workspace must optimize review efficiency without compromising governance, queue visibility, or business throughput.
