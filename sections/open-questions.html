Block 8 — Delivery Strategy

Open Questions & Decisions Required

Key questions this PRD does not yet fully resolve. Each requires alignment across Product, Engineering, Ops, Compliance, Security, and Leadership. Grouped by capability so decisions happen in the right forums.

Top 8 Questions for Leadership

The most critical unresolved decisions before Phase 1 can proceed:

  • 1
    What exactly is Phase 1 scope?
    Pure reviewer copilot, or broader workflow and product transformation?
  • 2
    What is the policy source of truth?
    And who owns policy codification and updates?
  • 3
    What are the safe automation boundaries?
    AI prepares only, or can it recommend, pre-decide, or auto-close in some cases?
  • 4
    What workflow system owns the case lifecycle?
    Existing platform versus this new product.
  • 5
    What audit depth is mandatory?
    Especially for model trace, evidence lineage, and override capture.
  • 6
    What is the build vs buy strategy for OCR, extraction, and LLM stack?
    Core cost, quality, and time trade-off.
  • 7
    How much platform reuse should be designed now versus later?
    Speed-to-deliver vs platform abstraction depth.
  • 8
    What outcome defines success?
    Productivity, quality, scalability, compliance, or a weighted mix.
